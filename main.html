Short summary

When Bhallaladeva conspires against his brother to become the king of Mahishmati, he has him killed by Katappa and imprisons his wife. Years later, his brother's son returns to avenge his father's death.

Prabhas : Amarendra Baahubali, the adoptive heir to the throne of Mahishmati (Telugu) / Magizhmathi (Tamil) and a respected prince by the people for his compassion and nobility. Mahendra Baahubali "Sivudu" (Telugu) / "Sivu" (Tamil) (son), Amarendra's son to Devasena who was born during his father's death and grows up to meet the rebel alliance that is combating Bhallaladeva.

Anushka Shetty as Devasena (Telugu) / Devasenai (Tamil), the younger sister of the king of Kuntala, who marries Baahubali. Her inclusion in the royal family and calling out Sivagami's and Palvaalthevan's/ Bhallaladeva's law leads to civil conflict in the kingdom and their exile
